ABOUT

ABOUT

Bibleo is an online room for reading. Featuring an online book shelf to store and showcase your e-books, Bibleo offers a pleasant and serene environment for you to read books. In the upcoming versions, this web room will provide you with features like sticky notes, flash cards, pomodoro focus timer and your own magic wand to set reminders and TODOs!

Overview

Upload new books on the bookshelf

The bookshelf of Bibleo will be your most friendly companion here but it has to be safe from being host to copyright content too :/ This is why you will have to upload links to your e-books uploaded to some cloud like Google Drive / Drop Box or any other site which has the e-book. Just head click on the 'Add' button visible in the bookshelf page and Bibleo will ask you for all necessary details about the book to be able to add it to the library. Register now and fill your bookshelf!

Grouping books and adding tags

Bibleo offers you the feature to add your own personalized tags to your uploaded books and to group them together in collections viz. Some of the built-in collections are
  • Favourites
  • To Read
  • Have Read
  • Reading now

Focused Reading

Bibleo has its own e-book reader - Calmly, which is by default where the e-books will open up when clicked. Calmly gives you a serene focused environment to grace the very essence of reading and learning. Customizable too, you can learn more about Calmly by using it!
